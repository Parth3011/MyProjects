Dosa

Ingredients

  • Rice(Chawal)
  • Urad Dal(Adad ni Daal)
  • Fenugreek Seeds(Methi na Dana)

Recipe(Process)

  1. Soaking:- Chokha ane adad ni daal ne pani ma char-panch kalak paladi raakho
  2. Grinding: Paladela chokha ane adad ni daal ne alag-alag pisine saras mixer banavo.
  3. Fermentation: Banne mixer ne mix karo, mithun umeero ane garam jagyae aath-baar kalak maate ferment thava do.
  4. Cooking: Garam tava par fermented batter patharine sonepadi rang thay tyan sudi sheko.

Punjabi Dish

Ingredients

  • Sarson ka Saag
  • Makki ki Roti

Recipe(Process)

  1. Sarson ka Saag:- Boil the mustard greens and spinach until tender. Drain the water and puree the greens in a blender. Heat ghee in a pan, add ginger, garlic, and green chillies. Saut√© until fragrant.
  2. Add the pureed greens, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook until the mixture thickens and oil separates. Serve hot with makki ki roti.
  3. Makki ki Roti:- Mix corn flour, wheat flour, salt, and water to form a dough. Roll out the dough into thick rotis.
  4. Cook the rotis on a tawa until they are cooked through and have brown spots. Serve hot with sarson ka saag and ghee.
